News and Updates

August 11th, 2020: Welcome to the new site! I built this from scratch, and am pleased to share it with you.

This year has been pretty shitty because of the pandemic. Silver linings- more time to spend on this site and music, global wide pollution levels have dropped significantly, everyones a hermit like me now.

New music on the way! I'm currently working on my electronic/acoustic hybrid album. Be expecting a single to be released by end of the summer. Also, i've got all of the songs written for my next Chronicles of Time album. This is gonna be a special album. I'll be getting some producing and engineering help from my good friend Joe Alston (of Milkweed). We're also gonna record the album at my family's ancestral home up in the 1000 islands. That should be ready to relase by end of the year. Thanks for listening and please show support by subscribing to my YouTube channel and following me on Spotify and Facebook/Instagram/Twitter. Till next time...
